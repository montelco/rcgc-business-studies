Meet the Dean

Meet Dr. Patricia Claghorn, Dean of Business Studies


Dr. Patricia Claghorn began her career with Gloucester County College in 2004 as the executive director of corporate and professional development in the Division of Continuing Education. She was promoted to the position of Dean of Continuing Education in 2005 and held that position until June 2012, when she was promoted to the position of Dean of Business Studies.


 
 
 

"Best of Biz" Winner 2015 and 2016

Preparing students for the workforce is Cooperative Education's mission


The Cooperative Education Program exists because of the partnership between Rowan College and the Business Community. This unique hands-on career awareness and exploration experience enhances the academic portion of a student’s degree program. This offers students the unique advantage of pursuing internships and cooperative education experiences to better prepare them for the workforce.


 
 
 

Career Coach

Helping you find the information you need for both school and the workforce


Career Coach is designed to help you find a good career by providing the most current local data on wages, employment, job postings, and associated education and training. You can browse through a variety of options such as Careers, Degrees, Career Assistant, Resume Builder, and more to discover what you are searching for! Business and Workforce Development

Facilitating professional development opportunities for areas of business


To increase career marketability, students have the opportunity to gain additional training with recognized industry by earning non-credit certifications offered though the Division of Continuing Education.This program is designed to give managers the knowledge they need to achieve productivity goals, build energetic, cohesive teams and effectively lead their workforce.
