​​​​Four-Year Academic Partners

Rowan College programs are structured to make transfer an option for any student. Within New Jersey, the College's partners accept 59 to 65 credits, depending on the major to which a student is transferring. For a course-by-course transferability evaluation of a Rowan College business program to another New Jersey school, visit njtransfer.org.

The following collegiate institutions and transfer options are currently available to Rowan College students:
